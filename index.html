Fun Facts About Dogs

Did you know?

Dogs have an incredible sense of smell—up to 100,000 times stronger than humans! They can recognize hundreds of words and gestures, making them highly trainable and intelligent. Certain breeds were specifically bred for unique tasks, like herding sheep or pulling sleds.
